About me

Personal Information

I am Onur Deniz Akan, a recent PhD graduate in Earthquake Engineering from IUSS Pavia and the University of Pavia, Italy. My research focuses on 3D numerical modeling of nonlinear seismic soil-structure interaction in masonry towers. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am passionate about hiking, photography, and exploring new cultures. I have traveled extensively across Europe and Asia, gaining diverse experiences and perspectives that enhance my personal and professional life. In addition to my love for travel, I enjoy coding and developing new computational tools in my spare time.
